Tim's Journey

Support guy aspiring to become a fullstack developer.


Originally I started off as a fitness fanatic doing coaching and personal training. I then wanted to go corporate and ended up moving to car sales. However, following a few years in the industry and some time to reflect during the covid19 pandemic, I remembered the love I had for computors and decided to take the risk of starting over entirely so that I could break into the IT industry.


Ofcourse knowing I did not keep in touch with technology much for the previous decade, the expectation was that my job prospects would be bleak. Luckily though, I got a break and was given the opportunity to work as an account support consultant for a web hosting provider which gave me some exposure to the development world and initiated my hunger to take things further.


This page is the first step of my journey to feed this hunger and become a fullstack developer
